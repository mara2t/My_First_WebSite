Reach out to LHMS by filling out the form in the Contact Us page. Try to be as detailed as possible when sending a request for a quotation.

A representative will review your request and get back to you with a quote or a time to review and go over your request.

3

Once the service is accepted and a deposit has been paid, our team will begin the process of completing your project.
