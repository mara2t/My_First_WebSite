LHMS was founded by individuals working as developers and designers. 
                        We saw a unique opportunity to offer reasonably priced home services
                            to clients that were paying inflated prices. As the Los Angeles 
                            housing market boomed and luxury property owners needed quick and 
                            affordable home staging services, we began gaining more and more
                            traction. Today we serve the Greater Los Angeles and its surrounding
                            areas. LHMS is proud to work with developers, agents, home owners 
                            and fellow Californians to improve homes and get properties sold.
